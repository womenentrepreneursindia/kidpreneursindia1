Welcome to Kidpreneurs India

Imagine, Create and Progress

In a world brimming with possibilities, today's Kidpreneurs are the architects of innovation and change. At Kidpreneurs India (KPI), we believe that the sky is not the limit; it's just the beginning for these ambitious young minds ready to aim big and achieve even bigger milestones.

Managed by the esteemed Women Entrepreneurs India, KPI serves as a rich resource hub dedicated to nurturing the skills and aspirations of budding entrepreneurs. Our mission is to empower each child, providing them with the tools and support needed to transform their dreams into tangible realities.

KPI is proud to have a diverse team of experts across various disciplines—be it creative arts, scientific exploration, literary expression, or more. Through our hands-on workshops, children engage in immersive learning experiences that encourage them to explore their innate abilities. Each workshop is designed to cultivate creativity, foster critical thinking, and promote a spirit of innovation.

At Kidpreneurs India, we believe in the unique potential of every child. Our programs are tailored to inspire the Kidpreneurs of today, equipping them with the knowledge and confidence to navigate their entrepreneurial journeys. Join us as we empower the next generation of leaders and creators.

Together, let’s turn imagination into reality and watch as our young entrepreneurs progress towards a brighter future.

Enroll your child in our entrepreneurship courses and allow them to discover their worth. The content, format, and activities ensure that kids “experience entrepreneurship” in a fun and constructive way!
